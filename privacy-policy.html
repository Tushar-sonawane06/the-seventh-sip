Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how The Seventh Sip collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our website and place an order.

Effective date: April 8, 2026

1. Information we collect

  • Name, phone number, and campus/drop location details you submit while ordering.
  • Order details, preferences, and communication records for support requests.
  • Transaction references received from payment partners for order verification.

2. How we use your information

  • To process, fulfill, and support your orders.
  • To share updates related to your order status and delivery.
  • To prevent fraud, resolve disputes, and maintain service quality.

3. Payment data handling

Card and payment instrument details are handled by secure payment gateway partners. We do not store full card or UPI credentials on our own systems.

4. Data sharing

We only share data with essential service providers required for payment processing and delivery operations. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

5. Data retention and security

We retain personal information only as long as needed for operational, legal, and accounting requirements, and take reasonable safeguards to protect your data.

6. Your rights

You may request correction or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at +91 72767 39369.
